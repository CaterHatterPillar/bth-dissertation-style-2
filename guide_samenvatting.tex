\samenvatting
According to both ILLC standards and UvA promotion regulations,
a chapter containing a summary in
Dutch of your dissertation should always be included.
It is specified by:
\begin{verbatim}
  \samenvatting
    <your Samenvatting>
\end{verbatim}
